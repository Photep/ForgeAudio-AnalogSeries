Decisions implemented — D-01: hybrid dirty-flag (audio thread keeps trigger detection, only publishes; GUI runs the fill). D-02: snapshot-at-trigger {morph, character, effective swingFrac, bleedLfo} via tear-free seqlock, bleedLfo captured at the trigger instant (not re-read live at paint).
