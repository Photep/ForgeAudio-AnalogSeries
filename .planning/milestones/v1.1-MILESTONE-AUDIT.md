## E2E Flows

### Flow 1: Clock Connect → Sync Display → Beat-Aligned LFO
User patches clock → SchmittTrigger detects edges → state machine ACQUIRING → LOCKED → SYNC badge fades in (blinking then solid) → Rate knob snaps to ratios → ratio label displayed → division-aware phase reset with crossfade → beat-aligned output.
**Status: COMPLETE**

### Flow 2: Clock Disconnect → Fade Transition → Free-Running
User unplugs CLK → instant state revert to FREE → freqSlew smooths frequency back to knob Hz → SYNC/ratio/BPM fade out over 200ms → Hz readout fades in → LFO continues without click.
**Status: COMPLETE**

## Non-Breaking Observations

These are informational items accepted during phase verification — not gaps or blockers:

1. **RATE-04 `/1.5` divisor rounding:** `round(1/0.667) = 2` means `/1.5` resets every 2 clock beats (triplet-feel). Musically reasonable, accepted during Phase 9 human verification.

2. **DISP-05 Hz display during transition:** Hz text reads raw knob value, not slewed frequency, during the 50ms transition. Audio output is correctly slewed. Cosmetic only.

3. **DISP-06 incoming clock BPM:** Display shows effective BPM (clock × ratio), not raw incoming clock BPM. Logged as future todo, explicitly out of scope for v1.1.
