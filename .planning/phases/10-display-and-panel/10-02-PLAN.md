<task type="checkpoint:human-verify" gate="blocking">
  <name>Task 2: Verify display overlays and panel label in VCV Rack</name>
  <action>
User visually verifies all Phase 10 display and panel changes in VCV Rack.

What was built:
- SYNC badge (top-right, blinks ACQUIRING, solid LOCKED)
- Ratio label (top-left, shows /4, x2, etc. when clocked)
- BPM readout (bottom-right, shows effective BPM when clocked)
- Hz readout (top-left, shows frequency when free-running)
- 200ms fade transitions between modes
- CLK label on panel SVG above CLK jack

Verification steps:

1. Panel label check:
   - Verify "CLK" label visible in lavender above the CLK jack (right side, same row as RATE)
   - Verify RATE label unchanged (white-gray, left side)

2. Free-running mode:
   - No clock connected -- verify Hz readout appears top-left (e.g., "0.70 Hz")
   - Turn Rate knob -- Hz value updates
   - Verify no SYNC badge, no ratio, no BPM visible

3. Clock connect (ACQUIRING):
   - Add a clock source (e.g., CLOCKED or BPM CLOCK module) and patch to CLK jack
   - Verify Hz readout fades out (~200ms)
   - Verify SYNC badge appears top-right and BLINKS (~2Hz fade in/out)
   - Verify ratio label appears top-left (e.g., "x1")
   - Verify BPM readout appears bottom-right

4. Clock tracking (LOCKED):
   - After a few clock pulses, SYNC badge should stop blinking and become SOLID amber
   - Turn Rate knob through different ratios -- ratio label changes immediately (e.g., "/4", "x2", "x8")
   - Verify BPM updates with ratio changes (e.g., 120 BPM clock at /4 = "30 BPM", at x2 = "240 BPM")

5. Clock disconnect:
   - Unplug the clock cable
   - Verify SYNC/ratio/BPM fade out (~200ms)
   - Verify Hz readout fades back in

6. Edge cases:
   - Very slow clock (e.g., 30 BPM) -- BPM should display correctly
   - Fast clock (e.g., 240 BPM) at x16 ratio -- should show high BPM
   - Division ratio /16 at 120 BPM -- should show "7 BPM" or "8 BPM"
  </action>
